Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

It is one of the oldest part of the complex. It was established by our 24th principal. Every sunday, all the students that are chirstian come here to pray in a congregation. On Christmas, the whole place is decorated with many lights candles and much more. It has been a part of the building since the begining



School Library

Was built in 1980

There more than a Thousand types book present for all the students to read and learn from. there five permanent staff for the library. Mr. Taposh is the librarian. There are books ranging from religious books to children's read. Each student has to get a library card before being able to take a book from the library. Other stationary items like pencils, pens, erasers etc. can also be bought.



School auditorium

Was built in 1982

There are seats available for all the students of the school. All the speeches are delivered here and any big announcements are given here. It has been a part of the complex for a long time. All the screenplayes , dramas and acts are practised and played here.
